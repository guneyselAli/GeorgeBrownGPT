
George Brown College Seniors' Association is a not for profit organization affiliated with George Brown College.
The Association is open to persons 55 and over, offering daytime programs of a cultural and educational nature that serve lifelong learning, needs and interests. General interest courses are held in fall and winter, usually 10 weeks in duration. Our Great Treasures lecture series takes place in spring and fall.
Our office is located at St. James Campus, 200 King St. E., Room -106.
For more information contact us at 416-415-5000, ext. 2418. or email us at senex@georgebrown.ca.
