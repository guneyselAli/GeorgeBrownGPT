
Administration at George Brown plays an important role in the smooth functioning of all aspects of the college.
The Senior Management Profiles introduce you to the strong team of leaders we have managing the college.
Finance provides you with all the relevant contact information for accounts receivable, accounts payable, financial services, financial planning, payroll and the bookstores.
The Facilities Management Department provides a comprehensive range of services such as space planning, the design, building construction, renovations, engineering, maintenance, energy management, housekeeping, furnishings, grounds maintenance and transportation. It is responsible for operating and maintaining buildings at all College Campuses: St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, Toronto Metropolitan University Campus and Waterfront Campus. Facilities provides health and safety guidelines for contractors working at the college to maintain the safety of everyone involved.
