
Enactus is an international non-profit student organization with active membership in 36 countries, including 70+ academic institutions across Canada. Members strive to achieve Enactus’ core values of social empowerment, environmental sustainability, and responsible entrepreneurship.
At Enactus George Brown, our mission is to change lives and shape a better, more sustainable community. With support from faculty advisors and industry professionals, Enactus George Brown’s student members can gain actual workforce experience and develop soft skills while contributing to various ongoing projects… even starting your own business or social enterprise!
