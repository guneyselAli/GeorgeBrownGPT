
You can apply online for a full-time program to attend George Brown College directly through the ontariocolleges.ca website.
Apply at www.ontariocolleges.ca, the application processing service is owned and operated by Ontario’s publicly-funded colleges. There is a non-refundable fee to apply (see ontariocolleges.ca for details).
