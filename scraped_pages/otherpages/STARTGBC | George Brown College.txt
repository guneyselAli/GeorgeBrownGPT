Whether you are a current student or an alumnus startGBC is your gateway to entrepreneurship at George Brown College. We are a physical and virtual hub. We connect students, alumni and the community with – resources, programs and support. Our goal is to foster a culture of entrepreneurship on campus and beyond.
We offer all students/alumni a fundamental toolkit of entrepreneurial best practices. Our focus is to help you develop entrepreneurial skills and grow from ideation to commercialization, providing support and guide your entrepreneurial journey.
At startGBC, we strongly believe – collaboration creates innovation, so go forth and – collaborate, create and innovate!
