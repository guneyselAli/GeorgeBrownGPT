Our goal is to build strong relationships between industry, our faculty and college, and of course, our students. We have created a community of industry partners, corporate supporters, dedicated instructors, students, and most important of all, a place where each of these groups can share ideas and open up doors of opportunity. Our priority is simple: to work with industry partners to determine exactly what skills students need now - and in the future - then give them the very best training to acquire those high-demand skills.
