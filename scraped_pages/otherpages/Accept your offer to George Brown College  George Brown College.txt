
To accept your offer, visit ontariocolleges.ca. You'll need your OCAS username and password. Once you log in, select the "Confirm" tab and choose your program. 
You can also confirm by calling the Ontario Colleges Application Service at 1-888-892-2228.
International students can accept their offers through the International Application portal.
You must respond by the expiry date noted on your offer.
