Did you know that you can borrow technology equipment from the library for free? All you need is to visit a Library Learning Commons with your digital or physical student ID (this is your library card) and complete a one-time Tech Equipment Loan Agreement form (accessible on campus).
View our equipment pages below to find out more about some of the amazing technology that is available to you as a GBC student!
Most of our tech equipment is housed at our St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront LLC locations, but some of our satellite locations also have select tech equipment available. Drop by an LLC location to discover what is available to you! To find out about something specific, please call ahead and speak with one our technology specialists.
