CELA, the Centre for Equitable Library Access, is Canada's most comprehensive accessible library service, offering books and materials in a variety of alternative formats to patrons with print disabilities. Offering nearly 400,000 accessible titles to students with print disabilities for free, collections include: business, health, culinary arts, disability studies, career/job seeking resources, award winners and best sellers, and more than 50 periodicals. Students can access materials using Daisy text, Daisy Audio CDs, braille, downloadable audio and ebraille and described movies.
