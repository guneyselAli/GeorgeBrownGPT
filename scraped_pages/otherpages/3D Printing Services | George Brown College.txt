3D printing services are now available to current staff and students
The St. James and Casa Loma LLC both provide 3D printing services using 5th generation MakerBot Replicators.
3D printing is available between Monday–Friday, 10am–4pm and costs $3 per hour, which must be paid in advance.
Depending on print demand, print jobs exceeding 4 hours may not be approved. Wait times will vary depending on the length of the queue.
You can make your own designs or download ready-to-print designs from websites such as:
The 3D printer may only be used for lawful purposes. Printing weapons, unsafe or offensive objects, sexually-explicit materials, and objects that violate the intellectual property rights of another person or group is prohibited. The library reserves the right to refuse any 3D print request. Only library staff will have hands-on access to the 3D printers.
