
Better Jobs Ontario has replaced the Second Career program. The program now offers increased eligibility for the unemployed and underemployed as well as those on social assistance, gig workers, youth and newcomers to Ontario. Please visit georgebrown.ca/better for more information. 
For specific Better Jobs Ontario related inquiries, contact the Community Partnerships Office. Better Jobs Ontario Team within the Community Partnerships Office provides student focused advising for Better Jobs Ontario funded students across the College. Advising is available in person, over the phone or email.
