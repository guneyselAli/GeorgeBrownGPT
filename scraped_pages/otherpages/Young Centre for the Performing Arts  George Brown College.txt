
The Young Centre for the Performing Arts is home to our Theatre programs.
For a tour of the Young Centre for the Performing Arts, please contact: (416) 415-5000, ext. 2167 or performingarts@georgebrown.ca
From King or St. Andrew subway stations on Line 1 (Yonge-University-Spadina), take the King streetcar east to Parliament Street and travel south to the Distillery District entrance. Or, from Castle Frank subway station on Line 2 (Bloor-Danforth), take the No. 65 Parliament bus and get off just south of Front Street.
Please note the theatre is located in the Distillery district which includes cobblestone streets with uneven bricks.
