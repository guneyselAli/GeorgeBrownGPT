
The Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System (WHMIS) Training is for our new First year School of Dental students only per academic year. You must register and pay for the WHMIS online course with Peak Excellence Shop only from their website at https://www.peakexcellenceshop.com/
Once you completed all the modules and pass this course, Peak Excellence Shop will issue you a WHMIS certificate and it is valid for two years after the issued date and you will need to submit and upload this certificate along with your completed Prerequisite Health Form documents to the ParaMed Placement Pass website by the specific program deadline indicated on your forms.
