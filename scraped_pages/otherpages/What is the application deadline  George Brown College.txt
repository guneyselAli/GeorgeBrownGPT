
Ontario Colleges begins accepting applications in October for the upcoming academic cycle (e.g. opens in October 2022 for programs beginning in Fall 2023, Winter 2024 and Spring 2024). You may apply at any time. However, the equal consideration date is February 1st of the academic year you are applying for. It is recommended that you apply and have everything in by this date for our highly competitive programs.
