Campus tours are for prospective students who are thinking about attending George Brown College. Tours show the common areas of each campus including the cafeteria, library, gym, student services, and other facilities.
Check out our George Brown College campuses from anywhere in the world!
The main subway stop for Casa Loma Campus is Dupont station on Line 1 (Yonge-University-Spadina). From Dupont station, travel north on Spadina Road to Macpherson Avenue and then travel one block west to Kendal Avenue.
There are pay parking lots located on Macpherson and Bridgman avenues. Find other parking lots around Casa Loma Campus at parkopedia.ca
