
This scholarship was established in loving memory of Anna Lopez, a member of the support staff team at the Centre for Construction & Engineering at George Brown College.
Anna loved the George Brown Community and supported students and staff with genuine care and enthusiasm. She demonstrated great strength and thrived in collaborating with everyone to work out challenges. Above all, Anna embodied generosity, kindness, grace, and compassion for others.
The Anna Lopez Memorial Scholarship is awarded to a student who has a good academic standing and has overcome or faced personal/financial obstacles to undertake their studies at George Brown. 100% of your donation will directly go to support the scholarship.
