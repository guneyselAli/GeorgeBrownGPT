
Looking to transfer to another college or university?
A George Brown College diploma or degree is a passport to further education at many local, national and international post-secondary institutions.
The Transfer Agreements and Opportunities Database offers pathways whether you have completed a semester, year, or are a graduate.
Contact the institution you wish to transfer to for more details about admission requirements, amount of time to complete, and number of transfer credit granted on admission to the program. 
Visit ontransfer.ca for more resources and information for transfer students.
