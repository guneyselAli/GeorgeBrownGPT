
This page outlines all you need to know when it comes to using Zoom. 
You might have a Zoom account associated with your personal email. If you do, you can switch between different Zoom accounts (GBC and personal) by using a different login.  
If you have used your GBC email to register a Zoom account before, you may need to follow the account reclaim process. If you encounter an issue contact helpdesk@georgebrown.ca 
On this page you will find these support topics: 
Your instructor may require you to login and authenticate your GBC credentials to join the meeting room. Doing so requires a GBC Zoom account. Click on "Sign In" on the https://georgebrown-ca.zoom.us/  portal to activate GBC Zoom account, and then sign into the Zoom desktop or mobile application using the Single Sign On (SSO) option.
We try to make this tool as accessible as possible Login to Zoom in many ways:
ZOOM DESKTOP APP ZOOM PORTAL ZOOM ON YOUR MOBILE

Meeting controls are dependent on how you access the Zoom lecture – using the computer or a mobile device. NOTE – not all controls may be visible; they may be disabled.
