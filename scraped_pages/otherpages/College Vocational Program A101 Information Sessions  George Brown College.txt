
Attend an online College Vocational Program A101 | George Brown College information session to learn how our program can help you to develop and strengthen your knowledge, skills and abilities to confidently enter the world of work or continue on your path of career development and training. Find out how the College Vocational Program has worked with a community partner to offer employment services and support during and after graduation.
Admission criteria, application, intake assessment and acceptance process will be discussed. Attendees will be provided with an opportunity to ask program staff questions.
The program runs in-person, Monday to Friday. The next start date for the College Vocational Program is September 2023.
Family, friends, teachers and/or support people are welcome to attend an information session.
To learn more about the sessions and the program, contact the department: College.vocational@georgebrown.ca
