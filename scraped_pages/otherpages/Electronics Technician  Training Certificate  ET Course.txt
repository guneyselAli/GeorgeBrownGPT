The Electronics Technician Certificate program can help you acquire the knowledge and skills you need to succeed in the field of high tech manufacturing.
The Electronics Technician Certificate program provides flexible, skills-based training in the fundamentals of electronics. It has been developed for adult learners pursuing training through independent study, specifically students who cannot attend college full-time because of work or family commitments. Included is the simulation software, CircuitLogix, which converts your computer into a virtual Electronics lab.
