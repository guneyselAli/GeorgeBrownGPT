
New Paramount Studios is retained through the Student Association.
New Paramount Studios is open for all graduating students.
Photographs will be taken at taken at the Waterfront and Casa Loma campuses, along with their studios in Toronto and Mississauga. To ensure that you are included into College Course Composites, please book your appointment early.
New Paramount Studios supplies all the graduation regalia for your photograph.
Please note: To appear on College Course Compositions you must have your photograph taken before June 1, 2023.
For additional information please visit New Paramount's website, email nps@look.ca or visit the Student Association's grad photo page.
