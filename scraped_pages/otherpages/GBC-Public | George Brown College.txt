
Visitors to George Brown College campuses and locations can use the GBC-Public Wi-Fi network. 
GBC-Public provides basic internet access for checking email or routine browsing. This network does not support high-bandwidth activities like streaming. 
For GBC students and employees, GBC-Public does not provide access to internal college resources.
The personal information you use to log in remains private—GBC will never sell or provide this information to third parties.
