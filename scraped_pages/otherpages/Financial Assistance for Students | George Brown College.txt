
We have resources available to help you pay for your college education. You can learn more about these resources below.
We understand that the financial side of college can be intimidating, but we're here to help. You can contact the Financial Aid Office at FinAid@georgebrown.ca with questions. We recommend using your GBC email which helps us verify your identity.
OSAP provides financial assistance through loans (money you pay back) and grants (money you don’t have to pay back).
Our awards program is designed to help students in financial need and those who do well academically. There are many awards offered through the college and external partners.
Sometimes things come up that we don’t plan for. When this happens, emergency funding may be available. Contact Financial Aid (FinAid@georgebrown.ca) to access the application.
Did you know Aeroplan, TD Bank or CIBC loyalty points can be turned into funds you can use on campus? You can ask family and friends to donate their points to help support your studies.
This program provides students with on-campus job opportunities for up to 15 hours a week.
Are you an applicant or current student with a Registered Education Savings Plan (RESP)? You may be eligible for the Canadian Learning Bond.
If government funding isn’t for you, there are other options that might fit you.
The payment plan is available to new and returning domestic students and returning international students. This allows student to pay their semester tuition in three instalments.
