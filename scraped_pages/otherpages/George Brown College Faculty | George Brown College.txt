
A range of service and tools available for George Brown faculty 
Career Services supports the Academic Centres through workshops, events, an online job board and resource materials. 
Cornerstone (My Learning) is the college’s professional development (PD) portal.
FacultyConnect is an online hub of videos featuring George Brown College's own faculty sharing concrete practices that they use to foster well-being in the college classroom.
iTAC (Instructional Technology Assistance Centre) provides support for classroom and event computers, projectors, screens, microphones, and more.
Partial Load faculty who have taught a specific course in a department or school have priority to teach that course again provided there has not been a substantial change in course.
The TLX supports excellence in teaching and learning, offering a wide range of services for faculty.
