
The Good Food Market at St. James Campus is a weekly non-profit fruit and vegetable market run in partnership with FoodShare Toronto.Â The market supports convenient, low-cost access to fresh, nutritious food, and advocates for student food security.
All students and GBC community members are welcome.
The GFM sends out optional pre-order forms every week. Email goodfoodmarket@georgebrown.ca to be added to the mailing list.
Dates: Thursdays (except intersession week)
Time: 11 a.m. to 3 p.m.
Location: St James Campus (SJA) - 200 King Street East - In front of the Bookstore (Campus Store)
