
At George Brown College, we offer you three great options to get to know us better and to tour our campuses, classrooms and labs.
Are you thinking about coming to George Brown College? A general campus tour is a great option for prospective students and their families. Our tour guides will give you an idea of what to expect from student life at George Brown, as well as show you classrooms, labs and common areas at each campus, including the cafeteria, fitness centre and athletics facilities, and student services, among others.  
Do you know what you’d like to study at George Brown? Our information sessions provide program-specific details for prospective students and may include tours of classrooms and labs. Our program coordinators and faculty lead these sessions and can answer your questions about specific courses, our facilities and career options.
Have you been accepted to George Brown? Be sure to register for our orientation sessions for new students. These events provide an opportunity to meet your teachers and other students in your program, tour your campus, receive your book lists and take part in some fun activities before you start classes.
