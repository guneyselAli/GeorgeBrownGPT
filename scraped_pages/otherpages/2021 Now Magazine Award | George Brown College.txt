
George Brown voted Toronto’s best college in 2021 NOW Magazine Readers’ Choice Poll
Readers of one of Toronto’s best-known publications have voted George Brown as the city’s best college for the second straight year.
The results of the 26th annual NOW Magazine Readers’ Choice poll were released November 11, and we’re excited to once again be named a favourite. In 2020, we were voted best college and best language school/classes (Continuing Education).
NOW Magazine is an alternative weekly that has been publishing news, events and reviews since 1981 — a publication that has long had its pulse on everything Toronto.
George Brown College offers 164 full-time programs and 180 continuing education certificates/designations with campuses located in downtown Toronto. We provide hands-on education with real-world learning experiences, and we work closely with industry to help our graduates hit the ground running in the workforce.
