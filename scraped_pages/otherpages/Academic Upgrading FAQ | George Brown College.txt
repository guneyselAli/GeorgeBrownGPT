Academic Upgrading is a tuition-free program for adults who want to improve their skills in reading, writing, math and science. Whether you want to take your courses in person or online, we will support you in building a learning plan to help meet your goals and you'll get career advice, counselling and other supports at George Brown.
Want to know more? Email us at upgradinginfo@georgebrown.ca, or give us a call 416-415-5000 ext. 3560.
Our courses help individuals meet college and apprenticeship entry requirements in English Communications, math and science. We also offer courses in computer skills, as well as personal and professional exploration. Courses are offered at varying levels up to the Academic and Career Entrance level, commonly known as ACE. ACE is a credit level accepted and recognized by Ontario colleges for admission purposes.
CHOICES at George Brown is a free, one-week program that helps you learn about yourself and set goals for the future.
By the end of the week, you'll be clearer about your career interests and goals. You'll have a plan of action and our coaches will help you take your next steps.

Your courses will depend on your program. Many of our courses are available both in-person and online. George Brown upgrading courses are offered in English, math and science. Different levels of English and math are offered, right up to ACE (Academic and Career Entrance). Science courses are offered at ACE (Academic and Career Entrance) level only. ACE credits are recognized by Ontario colleges for application to postsecondary and apprenticeship programs. Many employers recognize the ACE Certificate.
