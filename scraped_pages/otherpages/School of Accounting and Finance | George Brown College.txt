
The School of Accounting and Finance offers a wide array of diploma, advanced diploma, degree and graduate certificate programs. Our programs offer field education components and are designed to prepare you for an ever-evolving world of business and finance.
Our practical approach to financial services studies blends business and finance to teach you all the skills you’ll need for the many diverse career options in the financial services sector. The financial services sector is critically important to the Canadian economy and of that of the Toronto region. A detailed review of the robust financial sector ecosystem can be found in The Financial Services Ecosystem in the Toronto Region report.
Our Entry Advising service is a free service provided by the college to help match your skills, background and career goals with program options.
