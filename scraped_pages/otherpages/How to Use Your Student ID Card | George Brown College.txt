
Always carry your student ID card when on campus as it’s required for accessing services,    participating in a number of academic activities and could be required for attendance in classes   and to write tests and exams.
The Athletic Centres at St James Campus and Casa Loma Campus require you to tap your valid student card to be granted access to the facility, and to submit your student card to use sports equipment. Remember to pick up your card when you return equipment.
Your GBC student card function as your borrower card at the Library Learning Commons (LLC) and is used to borrow books, A/V equipment, Laptops and other electronic devices, as well as a print/copy card.
Your GBC student ID card may be requested to take your tests and or exams.
