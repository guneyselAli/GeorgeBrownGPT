
George Brown students have the opportunity to complete work-integrated learning abroad! This means you’ll get to learn by doing but in an international setting. Having global work experience will make you even more desirable as a potential candidate and positively affect your resume and prospective career.
If you are a current George Brown student and would like to complete a work-integrated learning (WIL) opportunity abroad, you can learn about the application process below.
Connect with your placement coordinator about the guidelines and processes necessary for your academic department.
Contact the International Centre’s Global Mobility team for instructions on how to apply, timelines and requirements by emailing globalmobility@georgebrown.ca
If you are planning on a WIL abroad, you may wish to register for the Cultural Intelligence Certificate the semester prior to your WIL abroad opportunity.
