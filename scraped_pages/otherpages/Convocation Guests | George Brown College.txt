Follow these steps if you want to invite a family member from overseas to convocation:
International Students – May request an optional Letter of Enrolment (if currently enrolled) OR Graduation letter (if program completed) from the International OfficeDomestic Students – May request an optional Letter of Enrolment through records.georgebrown.ca, or optional graduation letter by emailing convocation@georgebrown.ca if they have graduated.
See more information on how to write the invitation letter at Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).
