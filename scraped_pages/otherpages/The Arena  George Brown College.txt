
Gear up for an exhilarating entrepreneurial showdown—the rebranded March Madness, now known as THE ARENA, is here, and it's more exciting and grander than ever for 2024!
The Arena Pitch Competition invites post-secondary students to showcase a diverse array of business ideas throughout March. Drawing inspiration from the NCAA March Madness basketball competition, this competition pits 64 student entrepreneurs from across Canada against each other in head-to-head brackets, all vying for the coveted title of the nation's best.
The application deadline for The Arena 2024 is approaching fast—January 20, 2024, at 11:59 pm AT. Don't miss your chance to be part of this thrilling entrepreneurial journey!
