Registration for January 2024 begins on Monday, November 13, 2023.
To find out if your program will be delivered online or as a hybrid (online + in-person), see Program Delivery for Winter 2024.  Visit our Health, Safety & Wellness page regarding on-campus health and safety information. 
All full-time post-secondary students must register online via STU-VIEW. You will need your student ID number and password to access your STU-VIEW account. If you have forgotten your password, please call the Contact Centre for assistance at 416-415-2000, 1-800-265-2002 (Toll-Free), or 1-877-515-5559 (TTY).
