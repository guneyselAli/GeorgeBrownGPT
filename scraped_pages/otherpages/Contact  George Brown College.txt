Our Career Advisors help students and alumni to explore and identify career interests, create personalized networking and job search plans, develop personal branding, have mock interview preparation for job interviews, and more via personalized one-on-one 45-minute virtual career advising appointments.
While Career advisors act as a point of contact for specific program faculty, they support all students from any program. Students and alumni can book appointments on GBCareers and faculty and staff can email our team directly or visit our faculty/staff page for more information about our supports.
Students are encouraged to connect with their program coordinators for specific industry-related expertise/advice. Staff and faculty are encouraged to connect with their respective advisors for support and collaboration request.
