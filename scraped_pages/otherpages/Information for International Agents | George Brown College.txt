
Students from more than 120 countries have chosen George Brown College for their education. 
We offer more than 160 career-focused certificate, diploma, post-graduate and degree programs - one of the widest ranges of full-time college programs in Canada. Located in downtown Toronto, Canada we can provide access to top employers for field education opportunities* through our network of industry partners.  
George Brown College maintains a network of authorized international agents who play an important role in aiding prospective international students through their application process while representing the College in a professional and trustworthy manner at all times.
