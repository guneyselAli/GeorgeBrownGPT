Students may apply for The George before they are accepted into their program as your residence application is separate from your College application. Please note, if you are not accepted into your program and wish to cancel your residence application you must do so before June 12, 2023. To cancel or withdraw simply log onto My Housing Portal and go to the "Cancellation Request" tab at the top.

My Housing Portal accepts payments from MasterCard, Visa, and many major banks.
Many major Canadian banks offer “George Brown College Residence” as a bill payee. *Please do not confuse this with “George Brown College” payee, which is reserved for tuition fees.
You may courier your payment in the form of a certified cheque/money order. Please do not send certified cheque/money by regular mail. Make all cheques payable to “CLC George Brown”. Address to 80 Cooperage St, Toronto ON, M5A 0J3 Attn To: Residence Manager.
PLEASE NOTE: you will not be notified if your payment has been received. For account information please check the Portal under the tab “Accounts” and you will be able to view your own account transactions online.
Please email info@georgebrownresidence.ca to receive Wire Transfer information.
