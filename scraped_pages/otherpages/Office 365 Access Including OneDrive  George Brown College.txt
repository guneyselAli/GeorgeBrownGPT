
George Brown College provides students and staff with free access to Office 365—a tool that allows you to create, edit and share documents and projects in real time and on the go.
Access to Office 365 including OneDrive is available to currently registered students and up to 1 year after your last registered course.
The service can be used on your PC or Mac and on your Android, iOS or Windows device. With your account, you can install the Office 365 application on up to five devices.
Students and staff can access Office 365 with your George Brown College login credentials.
