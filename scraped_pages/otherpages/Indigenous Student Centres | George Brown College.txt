
“The Indigenous student centre at Casa Loma is great. It has computers, a coffee machine that I use all the time and a free printer, which is super helpful.” - Douglas Tynes, First year George Brown College student, Fashion Management program
Everyone needs a quiet and comfortable place to study. George Brown College Indigenous Initiatives has THREE centres that provide:
SahkitchewaySt. James campusRoom 562D200 King St EToronto, Ontario
WichihitowinCasa Loma campusRoom 434C160 Kendal Ave.Toronto, Ontario
Jiigibiik nibwaakaawin tegWaterfront campusRoom 22751 Dockside Dr.Toronto, Ontario
At Sahkitcheway, Wichihitowin and Jiigibiik nibwaakaawin teg you can meet other students and study while having access to job postings, current event listings, community newsletters and college and university information.
