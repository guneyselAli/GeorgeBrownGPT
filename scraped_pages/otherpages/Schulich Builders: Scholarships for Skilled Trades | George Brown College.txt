
Schulich Builders Scholarships are awarded to students enrolling in full-time certificate and diploma programs within eligible skilled trade fields at participating Ontario colleges. Colleges offer the Schulich Builders Scholarships in programs with the most in-demand jobs in the skilled trade sector in Ontario.
The Schulich Builders program will award 100 scholarships annually with 10 scholarships available at each participating college. Schulich Builders Scholarships are valued at up to $40,000 ($20,000 per academic year), supporting the costs of tuition, living expenses and tools. Schulich Builders will benefit from internal resources at the college to enhance their student experience and assist in their employment within their relevant field in the skilled trades upon graduation.
Schulich Builders Scholarships aims to recognize and enable Ontario’s future skilled trade workers, with an annual investment of over $3 million through top Ontario colleges.
The sign-up deadline date is June 30, 2023.
