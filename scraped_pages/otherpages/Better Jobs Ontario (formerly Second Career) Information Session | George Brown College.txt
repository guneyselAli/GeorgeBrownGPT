
Interested in applying for Better Jobs Ontario, but don't know where to start? The Better Jobs Ontario Information Session is designed to guide you through the process. Second Career is an Ontario government-funded program that helps laid-off workers with skills training for jobs in growing fields. 
First, find out if Better Jobs Ontario is right for you. You can take this step before our information session by following the guide from the Ontario Ministry of Training.
Open to: Domestic applicants (Canadian citizens or permanent residents)Duration: Approximately 1 hourCost: Free
For more information, contact us at betterjobsontario@georgebrown.ca
Please note: attending this session is not required to apply. If you are ready, see the application guide for domestic students.
