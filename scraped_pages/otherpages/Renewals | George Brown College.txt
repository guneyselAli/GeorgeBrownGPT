You can renew your library item(s) if:
If you would like to renew an item you have checked out, you can do so online using your library account. For your login ID, append @georgebrown.ca to your student/employee number. For example, if your student number is 123456789, you would use 123456789@georgebrown.ca. Your password is your STUVIEW password. 
You can also renew in person or by phone.
