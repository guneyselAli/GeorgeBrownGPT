
It is with profound sadness that we announce the sudden and unexpected passing of our beloved Melissa Quan on Monday, May 14th, 2018 in Toronto at the age of 39. She was the cherished daughter of Ron Quan and Salvacion Quan (deceased, nee Curammeng) and loving stepdaughter to Myrna (Velasco-Quan). Predeceased by her devoted grandmother Edna Quan. Loving partner of Derek Bartoschek, niece to Elena (Quan-Musto), Sharon (Quan-McGimpsey) and cousin to Amanda, Charles (Musto) and D’Arcy (Quan-McGimpsey) and stepsister to Daniel, Sasha and Emma (Michael). She was loved by relatives and friends who span the globe.
Melissa Quan’s family and friends have donated funds for the establishment of the Melissa Quan Memorial Endowment. This award honours Melissa’s legacy, her fun-loving nature and passion for games.
