George Brown College’s Centre for Health Sciences programs, faculty and facilities reflect the trends and changes taking place in the industry. Our students enter their field with the most current training and the ability to work with colleagues from various disciplines to deliver high-quality, interprofessional care.
We offer certificate, diploma, degrees and postgraduate programs, as well as a Pre-Health Sciences program for those looking to strengthen their academics and general knowledge. Our Bachelor of Science in Nursing is a collaborative nursing degree program offered between George Brown College, Centennial College and Toronto Metropolitan University
