Program Description
Program Overview
In the 16-week
Apparel Fit Technician
certificate program, you'll learn how to recognize good apparel fit, identify fit problems, correct fit in ready-made garments, and apply ideal fit standards across different body types and fabrics. As a student in this program, you'll also develop your customer service skills.
Full Description
As a student in the 16-week
Apparel Fit Technician
certificate program, you'll have the unique opportunity to learn skills that are in demand in alteration, retail, bridal, costume, and custom-fit studios. Master the art of diagnosing fit issues in ready-made garments, recommending adjustments to correct fit, and marking garments for alterations. We'll also equip you with customer service skills and knowledge of different fabrics, ensuring you're ready for the workforce.
Classes take place at our vibrant hub, Fashion Exchange (FX), at
63 Regent Park Blvd
. FX is more than a learning lab; it's also a production facility readily accessible to local designers and fashion producers where you will be immersed in day-to-day apparel manufacturing operations.
